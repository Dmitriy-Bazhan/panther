How does the booking process work? Click to show more
When you book a nurse on our site, you specify exactly what you want to book them for. That is, you specify your wishes in the areas in which Pflege Panther is active. Here you also have the option of specifying whether you prefer a female or a male nurse. With another click, your booking request is sent off. Our matching system then searches the database for nurses who offer exactly what you are looking for, allowing you to select an individual nurse. The matched nurse is informed about your request and asked to accept it. The first nurse who meets your requirements and accepts your assignment will contact you by phone. This is usually within 24 hours of your booking request.
